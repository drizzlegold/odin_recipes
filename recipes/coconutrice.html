COCONUT-RICE RECIPE

Description

Every time a Nigerian mum says there’s rice at home, she means it.
And while rice can get boring, there is more than one way to enjoy this meal, including coconut rice.  So how do you make Nigerian coconut rice at home? This recipe should help.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Tomato Stew
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chicken/turkey or any protein of your choice
  • liver
  • Chilli pepper
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Stock cubes
  • Thyme
  • Vegetables; carrots, green peas , green pepper

Steps:

  1. Start by parboiling rice in a pot and cook your meat and liver as well cause you’ll need the stock.
  2. Fry the meat. 
  3. Fry the vegetables
  4. Add chicken stock, tomato stew and coconut milk in a pot to boil. 
  5. Next, add the parboiled rice, spice with some salt and pepper and the fried vegetable.
  6. Cover the pot and cook on medium heat. This way, the rice does not burn before the water dries up.
